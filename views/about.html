What is PAMI

PAMI is Pingora Asset Management's private network designed as a means of communicating corporate and employee specific information, as well as centralizing documentation and automating business processes.

Who should use PAMI

PAMI is designed for current employees of Pingora Asset Management.

What information can I find on PAMI

The following information can be found on PAMI:

  • Snapshot of payroll and paid time off (PTO) balances
  • Link to Pingora's external payroll and PTO systems
  • Pingora's employee handbook
  • List of employee discounts available
  • List of Pingora's policies and procedures
